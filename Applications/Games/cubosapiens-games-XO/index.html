HOW TO PLAY
  • Take turns placing your mark (X or O) on the 3x3 grid.
  • First to get 3 in a row — horizontally, vertically or diagonally — wins!
  • Click any empty square to make your move.
  • Keyboard: 789 / 456 / 123 matches the numpad grid layout.
  • Press R to reset the current round quickly.
  • Hard AI uses Minimax — plays perfectly. Best case is a draw!
  • Easy AI plays randomly — beat it every time!
  • Switch to VS FRIEND for local two-player on the same device.
  • Scores carry across rounds. Hit NEW SERIES to reset everything.
