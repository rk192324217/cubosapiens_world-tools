NEON ARENA

HOW TO PLAY
  • DESKTOP: A/D or Left/Right Arrow Keys to Move. Space or Left Mouse Click to Shoot.
  • MOBILE: Left Joystick to Move. Right Joystick/Tap to Shoot.
  • Shoot enemies descending from the top.
  • Build your multiplier by killing enemies without taking damage.
  • Taking damage resets your multiplier and costs HP. 3 hits and game over!
