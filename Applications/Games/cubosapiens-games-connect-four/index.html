HOW TO PLAY
  • Click on a column to drop your token.
  • Tokens fall to the lowest available space within the column.
  • The game continues until the board is full! Earn 1 point for every 4 tokens in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
  • The player with the most 4-in-a-row segments at the end wins!
  • In SOLO mode, play against the computer. In DUO mode, play against a friend on the same screen.
